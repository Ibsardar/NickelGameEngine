pivot                   : [x,y]     turning/scaling/positioning center point of sprite
siz                     : [w,h]     scale of image relative to its parent
rot                     : degs      rotation of image relative to its parent
larm                    : [x,y]     joint of left arm on body
rarm                    : [x,y]     joint of right arm on body
farm                    : [x,y]     joint of forearm on left/right arm
hand                    : [x,y]     joint of hand on forearm
back                    : [x,y]     joint of back on body
tail                    : [x,y]     joint of tail on back

May be applied only if image is a body (root Limb):
pos                     : [x,y]     initial topleft position
ctr                     : [x,y]     initial center position
rot                     : degs      initial cc rotation
scale                   : [w,h]     initial percentage scale (0=0%,1=100%)
